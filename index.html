About

Sun Clock is a 24-hour clock that displays the position of the sun, and sunrise, sunset, and twilight times for your current location.

Civil Twilight occurs when the sun is less than 6º below the horizon. The sky is bright enough that artificial light is not needed.

Nautical Twilight occurs when the sun is between 6º and 12º below the horizon. The horizon is still visible, and sailors can take sightings of stars for navigation.

Astronomical Twilight occurs when the sun is between 12º and 18º below the horizon. The sky is dark but the faintest stars will not yet be visible.

Golden Hour is the period shortly after sunrise or before sunset, when the light is redder and softer than when the sun is higher in the sky.
